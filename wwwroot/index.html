FAQ

Common questions your lecturer or reviewer may ask

Why separate Admin and Employee interfaces?
Because the data density, navigation goals, and permissions are very different. Separate interfaces scale better when records become larger and workflows become more specialized.
Why keep a shared login page as well?
A shared login simplifies entry into the system and automatically routes users by role, while separate login pages still remain available for direct access and testing.
What makes this more than a simple CRUD project?
The project includes role-based workflows, leave approval logic, payroll generation, self-service pages, dashboard analytics, and connected admin-employee interactions.
